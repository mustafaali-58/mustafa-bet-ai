# Beehiiv Expands into Podcasting: Aiming for Patreon

Podcasting has become an increasingly popular content medium in recent years. In 2023, Beehiiv entered this arena with the aim of attracting creators away from competitive platforms like Substack and Patreon. One of its most compelling features is its decision not to take a cut of revenue.

This strategy provides an enticing alternative for many creators, allowing them to have a greater earning potential while sharing their content. By adopting this approach, Beehiiv is becoming an attractive option for investors and content creators alike.

Furthermore, Beehiiv’s suite of tools and conveniences enables content creators to manage their businesses more efficiently. Their expansion into the podcasting domain is part of innovative transformations within the Fintech and digital content ecosystem, promising to offer increased opportunities for a wider array of content creators.
