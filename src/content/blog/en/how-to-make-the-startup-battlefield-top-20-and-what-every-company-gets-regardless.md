# How to Make the Startup Battlefield Top 20 — and What Every Company Gains

Startup Battlefield is a coveted platform for many entrepreneurs aiming to showcase their innovations. While the universal goal of every founder is to reach the Disrupt Main Stage, understanding what precedes this opportunity is crucial. Key preparations undertaken well before the main event can significantly increase the chances of selection for participants.

Notably, Startup Battlefield offers more than just a chance to step onto the stage; it provides invaluable resources to every participant. A core component of this process is effectively narrating your story and presenting your product compellingly. Applicants must clearly articulate how their solutions address pressing challenges and aim to connect with potential investors.

Every company presents itself on an equal footing at this platform, which offers opportunities for mentorship, networking, and visibility. The return on investment extends beyond mere exposure; the impact these startups have on the ecosystem is also tremendously significant. Therefore, the journey into Startup Battlefield presents substantial opportunities for every entrepreneur involved.
