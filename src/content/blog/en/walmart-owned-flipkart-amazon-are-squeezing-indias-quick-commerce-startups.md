# Walmart-owned Flipkart and Amazon Squeeze India's Quick Commerce Startups

In recent years, quick commerce ventures in India have witnessed explosive growth. However, the strategic moves by major players like Walmart-owned Flipkart and Amazon are putting significant pressure on these burgeoning startups. 

Flipkart's push to expand beyond major urban centers, coupled with its aggressive discounting strategies, is creating a formidable competitive landscape. Analysts have noted that this scenario poses substantial risks for local startups, leading to concerns about their viability and profitability in an increasingly price-competitive environment. 

As the dynamics of the growing market evolve, it becomes critical for these quick commerce businesses to innovate and find sustainable solutions that enhance efficiency and customer satisfaction. Identifying ways to maintain a competitive edge in this rapidly changing landscape will be paramount for the future of the quick commerce sector.
