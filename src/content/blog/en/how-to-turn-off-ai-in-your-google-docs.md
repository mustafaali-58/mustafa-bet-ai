# How to Turn Off AI in Google Docs

In today's digital landscape, many software applications leverage artificial intelligence to enhance user experiences. However, these tools can sometimes become intrusive, especially when they manifest as notifications that disrupt your focus. With the recent updates to Google Docs, prompts like "write with Gemini" can be particularly bothersome for users. In this post, we will outline the steps to disable these AI features, allowing you to regain control over your document writing environment.

## Step 1: Access the Settings

Open Google Docs and click on the settings icon located in the upper right corner, typically depicted as a gear icon. From the settings menu, navigate to the 'Assistance Features' tab.

## Step 2: Disable AI Features

Within the settings page, you'll find options related to AI functionalities. To disable the "write with Gemini" feature, ensure you uncheck the relevant box. After this action, you should no longer receive notifications related to this feature.

## Step 3: Refresh Your Document

Once you're done configuring your preferences, refresh your Google Docs document. This step will ensure that your changes are applied effectively.

## Conclusion

While AI-powered tools can provide significant advantages while writing, there are times when personal preferences and work styles call for their deactivation. Turning off AI notifications in Google Docs is a straightforward yet effective way to create a less distracting workspace, allowing you to focus on what truly matters: your content.
