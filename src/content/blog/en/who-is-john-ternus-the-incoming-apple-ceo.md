# John Ternus: The New CEO of Apple

Tim Cook is passing the baton to John Ternus, after an illustrious 15-year tenure as the CEO of Apple. Ternus, with extensive experience in hardware engineering, has played a pivotal role in shaping Apple's innovative products throughout his career. 

His leadership style may introduce a new dimension to Apple's engineering culture and product development processes. During Cook's era, Apple not only expanded its flagship iPhone and iPad lines but also strengthened its service offerings. Ternus’s background suggests a broad expertise in both hardware and software integration, which could be invaluable moving forward.

This transition period raises crucial questions about how Apple’s strategies and market dynamics will evolve. With the rollout of the iPhone 15 and other hardware projects on the horizon, Ternus will face significant responsibility in shaping Apple’s future. This change signals a transformation process that investors and tech enthusiasts alike will be keenly observing.
