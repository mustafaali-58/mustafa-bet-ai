# The Importance of Enterprise AI at VivaTech 2026

In recent years, while Silicon Valley has aggressively pursued large language models and consumer-facing AI products, many European companies have been focusing on applying AI to the complex systems already embedded in everyday life. This trend is expected to become even more pronounced at VivaTech 2026. Enterprise AI is becoming a crucial tool for optimizing business processes, accelerating decision-making, and enhancing data analytics capabilities.

Many experts argue that Europe still retains a competitive advantage in this field, highlighting that AI should be considered not only as a technological innovation but also in the context of ethics and sustainability. Enterprise AI applications can help businesses operate more efficiently while also contributing to goals such as social responsibility and reduction of environmental impact.

VivaTech 2026 will offer participants significant opportunities for insights into technical innovations, shifts in business models, and the integration of AI applications. The various panels and workshops scheduled at the event will provide a chance to explore how European companies are leveraging AI technologies to gain competitive advantages.
