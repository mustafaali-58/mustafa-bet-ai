# Bluesky Embraces AI with Attie: An App for Building Custom Feeds

Bluesky has introduced its new app Attie, which utilizes the power of the open social networking protocol atproto to help users build personalized content experiences. Attie allows users to create a tailor-made feed populated with information based on their interests and social circles. The application of artificial intelligence (AI) in this context holds the potential to reshape the future of social media engagement.

Attie's advanced algorithms analyze individual user preferences and provide relevant content, enhancing not just user experience but also enriching social interactions on the platform. This move by Bluesky is evidence of how AI can be a transformative force in making social media experiences more meaningful for users.

Looking ahead, the proliferation of AI-driven social media applications is expected to bring forth more user-centric and customized experiences. Attie stands as a frontrunner in this transformation.
