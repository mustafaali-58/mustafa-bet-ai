# Why a Former AirPods Engineer is Now Building Heat Pumps

Merino Energy stands out as a company seeking to transform heat pump technology. A former AirPods engineer has shifted focus towards developing innovative solutions aimed at reducing the costs and simplifying the installation processes of heat pumps. This new approach represents a significant step not only towards enhancing energy efficiency but also contributing to efforts against climate change.

While heat pumps are recognized for their potential in energy savings, their complex designs and high costs have hindered widespread adoption. By taking steps to change this, Merino Energy is embracing a user-centric design philosophy. Drawing from engineering experience in the tech sector, there’s potential to create groundbreaking solutions for a substantial market.

Facilitating access to more affordable and easier-to-install heat pumps will yield significant benefits for both individual consumers and industrial applications. The future is being shaped by the innovative mindsets of companies and engineers like Merino Energy.
