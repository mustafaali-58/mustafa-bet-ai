# WhatsApp Disrupts New Spyware Attacks Linked to NSO Group

In a significant revelation, WhatsApp has announced that it has disrupted a phishing campaign aimed at its users, connected to spyware developed by the NSO Group. This incident underscores a major threat to user privacy and highlights the ongoing legal challenges faced by NSO as it allegedly violated court orders pertaining to its spyware operations.

WhatsApp's security team continuously works to combat such threats and implements various measures to protect user data. The recent attacks by NSO Group involved hacking predetermined accounts and disseminating malicious software through them. These types of spyware aim to infiltrate individuals' private information.

It is crucial to increase awareness among users regarding how to guard against such threats. WhatsApp advises that users keep their software updated and remain vigilant against messages from unknown sources. Ultimately, these cybersecurity attacks pose significant risks not only to corporations but also to individual users, making it imperative to remain informed and cautious.
