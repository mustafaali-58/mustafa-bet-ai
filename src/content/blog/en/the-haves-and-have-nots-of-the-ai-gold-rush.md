# The AI Gold Rush: Inequalities and Future Vision

The current boom in artificial intelligence (AI) presents a hopeful outlook for many; however, it is evident that even within the tech industry, positive vibes are in decline. In this article, I would like to focus on the opportunities brought by AI and the inequalities surrounding access to these opportunities.

In recent years, the technology industry has unearthed a significant AI goldmine. However, it must be pointed out that this victory belongs to only a limited group. Meanwhile, most of the technological advancements have concentrated among large companies and investors, leaving smaller startups and individuals with fewer resources in the periphery.

Having access to the benefits of AI requires not only technical knowledge but also the availability of financial resources and a wide network structure. This situation creates an increasing divide, building a chasm between the 'haves' and 'have nots'.

Looking towards the future, taking necessary steps to reduce this inequality is inevitable. Expanding education and resources to a broader audience is crucial for establishing a fair AI ecosystem. Allowing everyone to harness these opportunities will not only support individual successes but also foster societal development.

In conclusion, while advancements in AI hold great promise, more effort is needed to ensure these opportunities reach everyone. Reducing inequalities will allow the positive impacts of technological transformation to be shared across larger segments of society.
