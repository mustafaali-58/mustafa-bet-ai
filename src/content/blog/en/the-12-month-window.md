# AI Entrepreneurship: Opportunities in the 12-Month Window

In recent years, the rapid advancement of artificial intelligence (AI) has opened new opportunities for entrepreneurs. However, these opportunities won't last forever. Many AI startups are currently emerging during a period when foundational models have yet to fully expand into their respective categories. In this post, we will explore the significance of this 12-month window and what it means for entrepreneurs.

Structural gaps and areas of AI yet to be fully explored allow entrepreneurs to innovate without facing an established market. However, this situation is not expected to last. As foundational models integrate into more fields over time, competition will intensify and threaten the position of startups in the market.

Therefore, it is crucial for entrepreneurs to think strategically and explore the opportunities that this window presents. Investments in niche markets, facilitated by AI’s ongoing development, can yield significant short-term returns. Still, achieving long-term success requires a focus on sustainable business models and innovation.

Entrepreneurs can capitalize on this 12-month period by engaging with potential customers and enhancing existing solutions. Furthermore, building proprietary foundational models or partnering with open-source solutions can be effective ways to maximize this window of opportunity.
