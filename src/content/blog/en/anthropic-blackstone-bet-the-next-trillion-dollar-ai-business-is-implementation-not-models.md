# Transitioning to AI Innovation: The Importance of Implementation

Recently, a significant shift has occurred in the world of artificial intelligence with investments from Anthropic and Blackstone. AI labs are now focusing not only on developing algorithms but also on the real-world applicability of these algorithms. The launch of Ode, backed by Anthropic, aims primarily to accelerate AI adoption within enterprises by embedding forward-deployed engineers inside these organizations.

This new business model holds the key to the next trillion-dollar market in the eyes of investors. Implementation is a critical step in truly unlocking the potential of AI. Beyond strong AI models, determining how these models are applied in businesses is seen as the biggest stride the industry can take toward progress.

Looking ahead, the presence of engineers inside businesses will play a significant role in both the proper application of AI solutions and the transformation of corporate culture. This approach can catalyze a merger of engineering and business practices, potentially speeding up the digital transformation processes of companies.
