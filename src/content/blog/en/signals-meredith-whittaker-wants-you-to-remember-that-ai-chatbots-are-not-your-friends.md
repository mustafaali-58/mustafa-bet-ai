# The Limits of AI Chatbots: They Are Not Your Friends

In recent years, AI chatbots have become an integral part of our lives. However, Meredith Whittaker of Signal is urging us to recognize the potential dangers of these technologies. "These are not your friends. These are not conscious beings. These are not sentient interlocutors," she states, highlighting a darker reality in the interaction between AI systems and humans.

These statements underscore that artificial intelligence lacks the ability to form emotional bonds, and that forming deep connections with them could be dangerous. It is essential for people to be cautious about the potential misunderstandings or misguidances that these bots may produce.

AI systems operate based on the data they represent and the algorithms that drive them, but this does not make them sentient beings capable of human-like thought. During interactions, it is crucial to remember that bots respond solely as they are programmed. Especially in areas such as financial services and customer support, we must acknowledge that the suggestions provided by these AI systems may not always be accurate or reliable.

The future may hold a world where artificial intelligence is further integrated into our lives. However, as adult individuals, it is our responsibility to comprehend the effects of AI on human psychology and to assess these interactions within a healthy framework. 

AI chatbots should not replace human interaction but rather enhance it. Therefore, it is critical to maintain vigilance regarding these new technologies that influence emotional attachment and trust.
