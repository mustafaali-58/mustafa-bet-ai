# Roblox Introduces Kids and Select Accounts for Safe Gaming Experiences

In recent times, the safety and age-appropriateness of online gaming, particularly for child users, has become a significant concern. In response to this need, Roblox has introduced two new account types: "Roblox Kids" and "Roblox Select." With this new system, users aged five to nine will be assigned to a "Roblox Kids" account, while those aged nine to fifteen will receive a "Roblox Select" account.

These account types are designed to restrict the games and chat features users can access, serving as a buffer against potentially negative online influences. The "Roblox Kids" account, tailored for younger users, ensures that content is more controlled, while the "Roblox Select" account allows slightly older users to enjoy a broader range of games and chat experiences. This strategy not only protects users but also enriches their gaming experience.

Roblox's initiatives serve as a crucial example for other platforms, setting a standard in the gaming industry. By prioritizing child safety, it becomes feasible to build a more robust foundation for the future of gaming.
