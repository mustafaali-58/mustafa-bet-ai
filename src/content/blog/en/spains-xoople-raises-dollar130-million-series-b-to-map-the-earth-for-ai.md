# Spain’s Xoople Raises $130 Million Series B to Map the Earth for AI

Spanish startup Xoople has successfully raised a substantial $130 million in Series B funding aimed at developing artificial intelligence applications for mapping the Earth. This investment is expected to play a critical role in advancing popular AI technologies in the mapping domain, paving the way for more efficient and capable AI-based mapping systems.

Additionally, Xoople has announced a strategic partnership with L3Harris to build the sensors for its spacecraft. This collaboration is set to enhance Xoople's data collection capabilities and signifies a significant step toward innovative outdoor solution development. This approach towards space technology could lead to revolutionary advancements not only in the field of computer vision but also across various sectors, including agriculture, climate change monitoring, and more.

In conclusion, Xoople's recent funding and partnership with L3Harris represents a vital step in building a more sustainable future for all. The potential applications of AI in mapping will accelerate the transformation of numerous industries and strengthen the interactions between humans and the environment.
