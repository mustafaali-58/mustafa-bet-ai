# Reed Jobs: A New Era in Cancer Treatment

Reed Jobs, choosing to focus on curing cancer rather than discussions surrounding his last name, is paving a new path in the biotech industry. In the nearly three years since we last spoke at TechCrunch Disrupt, his venture, Yosemite, has grown rapidly into a team of 17. The biotechnology sector is on the brink of newfound opportunities as several blockbuster drugs face patent expirations in the coming years.

Jobs noted, "I didn't expect Yosemite to be moving this fast," highlighting the speed at which his organization has evolved. Artificial intelligence has transitioned from a mere curiosity to a significant component of Yosemite’s operations, presenting revolutionary potential in the biotech space. Reed Jobs' emphasis on cancer treatment reflects a personal struggle and a desire to create societal change, making the future of health technologies and AI integration an exciting frontier to watch.
