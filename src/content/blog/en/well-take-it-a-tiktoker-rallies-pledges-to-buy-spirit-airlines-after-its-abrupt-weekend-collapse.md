# A TikToker's Race to Buy Spirit Airlines After Its Sudden Collapse

In a remarkable turnaround facilitated by social media, a TikToker recently tapped into the collective power of the internet to seek financial backing for Spirit Airlines after its abrupt collapse. The user swiftly launched a rudimentary website, which took only an hour to create. Within this time frame, 36,000 'founding patrons' pledged nearly $23 million, overwhelming the servers in the process. This instance serves as a potent reminder of how platforms like TikTok can mobilize support for financial endeavors in times of crises. The ability to gather community-driven financial resources rapidly holds profound implications for how we might respond to corporate instability in the future, potentially marking the onset of a new kind of digital activism.
