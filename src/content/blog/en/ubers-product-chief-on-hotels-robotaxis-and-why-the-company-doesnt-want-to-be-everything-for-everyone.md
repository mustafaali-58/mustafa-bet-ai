# Uber's Product Chief on the Future: Robotaxis and Financial Services

Sachin Kansal, Uber's Chief Product Officer, discussed the company’s ambitions in financial services and its complex relationship with Waymo during a conversation with TechCrunch. The new Autonomous Vehicle (AV) Labs data operation of Uber highlights how AI is beginning to impact the experiences of riders and drivers.

Uber aims to expand by venturing into new areas such as robotaxi services and hotel bookings. However, as Kansal emphasized, the company does not aspire to be “everything for everyone.” Instead, it aims to create competitive advantages and enhance user experience by specializing in specific services.

The financial services sector plays a critical role in Uber’s plans, as the company seeks innovative solutions to reach more users and diversify its offerings. AI technologies, in this context, play a significant role and present an opportunity to deliver a smarter, more responsive, and user-friendly Uber experience for both drivers and riders.
