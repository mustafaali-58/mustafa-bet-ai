# Instagram Alerts Users Targeted by Hackers During AI Chatbot Attacks

Recently, Instagram has reported that users have fallen victim to cyberattacks facilitated by AI-powered chatbots. Although Meta has stated that it has taken steps to address the issue and fix the chatbot, some accounts are still being compromised by hackers. This situation highlights the necessity for enhanced security measures on the platform and the importance of raising user awareness regarding online safety.

Research indicates not only an increase in hacking attempts but also how social engineering methods exploit the vulnerabilities of online users. It is critical for users to take preventative measures to safeguard their account information. In this context, employing additional security measures, such as two-factor authentication, is highly recommended. While Meta’s efforts and improvements concerning AI-powered solutions are worth monitoring, users must also pay attention to what steps they can take in ensuring their own security.
