# Lachy Groom to Back Pronto: A Leap in India's House Help Market

Recently, the Indian house help startup Pronto has captured attention with the backing of Lachy Groom. If this funding round occurs, the company is poised to reach a valuation of $200 million within a matter of weeks. This doubling of its valuation serves as a testament to Pronto's remarkable progress.

India holds immense potential in the tech landscape, with the house-help service sector rapidly expanding. Pronto is solidifying its role in this growing market, attracting the interest of investors while also responding to the needs of service providers.  

Groom's investment in Pronto will not only provide financial backing but also contribute to enhancing the company's growth strategies and fostering innovation. Moreover, Pronto's emergence as a widely accepted platform presents a crucial opportunity for the proliferation of its products and services.
