# Cascade Raises $3.5M to Help Construction Firms Find and Win Projects

Cascade has successfully completed a $3.5 million seed funding round aimed at facilitating the project acquisition processes for construction firms. Notable investors, including A16z Speedrun, Ada Ventures, and Snowball VC, have participated in this investment round.

The funding is intended to empower construction companies to identify projects more swiftly and efficiently within an increasingly competitive marketplace. While traditionally seen as a conservative sector, the construction industry possesses significant potential for digitization and productivity enhancements. Cascade’s platform aids firms in locating the most suitable projects and increases their chances of winning those projects.

Such investments signify a rapid digital transformation in the construction industry. The digitization of business processes not only enhances operational efficiencies but also provides wider market access for construction firms. Cascade is making strides to carve out its place in the industry and is garnering attention for its future growth potential.
