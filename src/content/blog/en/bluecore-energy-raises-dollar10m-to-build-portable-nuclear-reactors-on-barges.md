# Bluecore Energy Raises $10M to Build Portable Nuclear Reactors

In recent years, the pursuit of making nuclear energy portable has opened new avenues for addressing the energy needs across various sectors. Bluecore Energy has taken a significant step forward by securing $10 million in a pre-seed funding round led by Slauson & Co. to build portable nuclear reactors.

The development of maritime nuclear reactors designed for use on barges could accelerate innovation in energy production. In light of growing pressures around climate change and sustainable energy solutions, portable nuclear energy systems have the potential to provide reliable resources as and when needed. Such innovative projects can transform the energy landscape, offering improved accessibility and efficiency in the long run.

Bluecore Energy's vision is aimed at democratizing nuclear energy by providing revolutionary solutions for maritime transport and local energy needs. With its engineering capabilities, the startup aims to deliver safe and sustainable energy production. These developments hold the promise of creating ripples across the energy sector, potentially finding wider applications in the forthcoming years.
