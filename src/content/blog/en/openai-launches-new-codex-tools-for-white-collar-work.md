# OpenAI Launches New Codex Tools for White-Collar Work

OpenAI is making a significant leap in courting enterprise users with the recent launch of new capabilities for Codex, announced on June 2, 2026. These tools are designed to broaden the application of Codex within the workforce, enhancing productivity and streamlining various tasks. Alongside the new capabilities, the company also released an internal report detailing how Codex is currently utilized by knowledge workers.

The report highlights that Codex is not limited to software development; rather, it has the potential to revolutionize daily operational tasks in businesses. The tools offered are set to automate routine duties for white-collar professionals, allowing them to focus on more creative and strategic tasks. For instance, with the support of Codex in coding, reporting, and data analysis, employees will have more bandwidth to tackle complex issues that require critical thinking.

In this context, it is anticipated that Codex will continue to gain traction in the workplace. It becomes crucial to contemplate how the integration of AI will transform employee roles and develop new methods of work in the coming years.
