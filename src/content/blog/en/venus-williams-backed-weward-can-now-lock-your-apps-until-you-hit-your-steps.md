# WeWard: Lock Your Apps Until You Hit Your Steps

The French app WeWard, backed by the renowned tennis star and angel investor Venus Williams, introduces an innovative solution aimed at encouraging users to increase their daily walking time. With a unique feature that locks your apps until you've reached your step goal, WeWard aims to boost walking time by nearly 25%.

This creative approach not only helps individuals integrate physical activity into their daily routines but also addresses the adverse effects of technology addiction. Users are prevented from accessing entertaining content or frequently used applications until they achieve a predefined step target, thereby encouraging them to take more steps.

Such applications not only benefit personal health but also foster social interaction and community building. Community events and challenges motivate users to heighten their physical activity and promote a healthy lifestyle. By offering this locking mechanism, WeWard strives to become both a facilitator of sports and an innovative source of motivation.
